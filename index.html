What Tagpo Is

The student platform for campus life.

Tagpo brings campus discovery, useful student tools, and local student perks into one verified app experience.

Built beyond social discovery. The Tagpo Card turns campus activity into check-ins, student promos, and verified partner access.

Verified students first.

Students sign up with accepted university emails, so campus communities stay focused on real students instead of random accounts.

Built around campus utility.

Students can discover events, organizations, notes, local promos, alumni gigs, internships, and nearby places that actually matter to campus life.

Social, but not only social.

Discover people, friends, and campus profiles while Tagpo also handles messaging, marketplace requests, org chats, confessions, and student access through the card.

Tagpo Card

The NFC card for student promos, event attendance, and verified campus access.

Students can still use Tagpo without a card. Public events, all-student promos, campus news, orgs, notes, marketplace, and social discovery stay open to verified students. The Tagpo Card unlocks the exclusive layer: card-only promos, partner experiences, attendance taps, and verified venue access.

Free in-app access Open to verified students

Browse public events, all-user promos, student orgs, notes, social discovery, and marketplace listings.

Tagpo Card access Unlock exclusive benefits

Tap for card-only promos, attendance, verified entry, and partner experiences that reset by venue rules.

  • Free promos and public events remain available inside the app.
  • The card unlocks exclusive promos, special events, and verified partner perks.
  • Students tap at partner venues to check into events or claim eligible discounts.
  • Promo usage is registered so one-use deals cannot be reused until they reset.
