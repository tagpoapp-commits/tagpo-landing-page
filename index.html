What Tagpo Is

Made for students who want real campus connection.

Tagpo combines the feel of a social dating app with the everyday tools students already need: events, orgs, notes, deals, confessions, and safer messaging.

Designed around real app screens. Events, promos, calendars, and campus activity are shown using the current Tagpo prototype UI.

Verified first, social second.

Students sign up with accepted university emails, so campus communities stay focused on real students instead of random accounts.

Built around discovery.

Find people by school, tags, interests, and spotlight activity, then unlock texting after a match or through approved school interactions.

More than matching.

Events, promotions, notes exchange, student organizations, marketplace listings, and anonymous confessions all live in the same app experience.

How It Works

From school email to campus community.

The flow is simple enough for first-time users and structured enough for a real verified student network.

1

Verify school

Choose a university and sign up with an accepted student email domain.

2

Create profile

Add photos, bio prompts, and hashtags across hobbies, interests, school life, and lifestyle.

3

Explore campus

Swipe profiles, check events, join orgs, browse notes, and save local student deals.

4

Message safely

Text after matches, notes requests, marketplace interest, or org membership opens a thread.

All Features

Everything students asked for, organized by use case.

Tagpo is not only a dating screen. It is a campus network with social, academic, marketplace, and safety layers.

01

Verified onboarding

Fake demo accounts for testing, plus the real concept of accepted university email domains.

02

Profile creation

Name, bio, hashtags, photos, school badges, and profile customization inspired by dating app profiles.

03

Discover matching

Pass, like, super like, match unlocks, profile detail drawers, and surprise next profiles.

04

Spotlight

A Discover tab that highlights the top five most interacted profiles from each university.

05

Events calendar

Join events, see upcoming and past signups, filter by distance or university, and browse campus news.

06

Promotions

Student discounts and local deals for food, clubs, coffee, and campus-area businesses.

07

Marketplace

Books, electronics, clothes, dorm items, and student-to-student buying or selling.

08

Notes exchange

Request notes, open details, send notes into message threads, and show how swaps or purchases work.

09

Organizations

Student org cards, join buttons, org details, upcoming events, announcements, and org chat.

10

Confessions

Anonymous campus confessions with trending, campus, following, likes, replies, and posting.

11

Messaging

Separate chat sections for dating, friends/social school stuff, notes, and org groups.

Moderation
